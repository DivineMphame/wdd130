Overview

Purpose

The main goal of this sport is to manuevr and navigate the raft through rapids or turbulent waters,often times taking you over rocks and drops,without capsizing the raft.In the past , traditional rafts were used to transport people and agricultural products.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website is adventure-seeking individuals and groups, aged 18-50, who are looking for an exciting and challenging experience on the water. They are likely to be physically fit, enjoy outdoor activities, and be willing to take calculated risks. They may be experienced rafters or newcomers to the sport, but all share a sense of excitement and a desire to push their limits.Scenario questions might include :

  • What is the primary motivation for our target audience to visit the water rafting website? Answer: To find information and book a water rafting trip that meets their desire for adventure and excitement.
  • What is the most important factor for our target audience when choosing a water rafting trip? Answer: The level of challenge and excitement offered by the trip, as well as the safety record and expertise of the guides.
  • How is our target audience likely to interact with the water rafting website? Answer: They will likely search for information on trip options, read reviews and testimonials, and book a trip online or contact the company directly to ask questions and make a reservation. They may also share their experience on social media and recommend the website to friends and family.
